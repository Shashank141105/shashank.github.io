My Favorite Stock Plugins in FL Studio

If you are one of those guys who say that good songs can only be made by costly third-party plugins, well here is a cool fact for you guys.

You don't need to go hard on your wallet to make sick beats. What you need are Stock Plugins!

And it is not only me saying it, even the king of drill beats, "Jay Cactus" thinks so.

My top 3 favorite VST plugins are:

  1. 3x Osc
  2. Patcher
  3. Gross Beat

Let's delve deeper into each of the plugins.

3x Osc

3x Osc in FL Studio is a powerful, versatile synthesizer with three oscillators for rich sound creation. It offers waveforms like sine, square, and saw, perfect for basslines, leads, and pads. It offers control knobs like tuning, panning, volume, and detuning, it delivers precise control, outshining many expensive plugins.

  • Tuning
  • Fine Pitch and Coarse
  • Panning

Patcher

Patcher in FL Studio is an incredibly powerful plugin that allows for advanced routing and combining of multiple plugins in one interface. It offers a modular environment where you can create complex chains of instruments and effects. With its visual workflow, you can easily manage and tweak your sound, making it an indispensable tool for creative sound design and efficient project management.

  • Advanced routing
  • Modular interface
  • Combine multiple plugins
  • Visual workflow

Gross Beat

Gross Beat in FL Studio is a powerful time manipulation effect that can create unique rhythmic and glitch effects. It offers real-time audio streaming and pitch-shifting capabilities, perfect for creating stutter effects, scratch effects, and gating. With its easy-to-use interface and creative potential, Gross Beat is a favorite among electronic music producers.

  • Time manipulation
  • Real-time audio streaming
  • Pitch-shifting
  • Stutter and scratch effects
